A Priority-Based Digital Platform for Fair and Timely Distribution of Critical Resources

ABOUT THE SYSTEM

The Essential Commodity Distribution System is a centralized digital platform designed to manage and monitor the allocation of critical resources in a fair, transparent, and priority-driven manner.
The system focuses on essential commodities such as LPG gas, medical oxygen cylinders, and fuel supplies, ensuring that urgent and high-priority requests are handled before general demands.

KEY SERVICES

LPG GAS DISTRIBUTION

LPG cylinders are distributed to registered users based on a structured queue system combined with priority rules. This ensures equitable access while supporting emergency and high-priority requirements.

MEDICAL OXYGEN SUPPLY

Oxygen cylinders are supplied exclusively to registered hospitals. Requests are treated as high priority and are processed immediately to support critical medical needs.

FUEL HOME DELIVERY

Petrol, diesel, and oil are delivered directly to users with an estimated delivery time calculation, similar to modern logistics and delivery systems.

SYSTEM FEATURES

  • Role-based access for Users, Suppliers, and Hospitals
  • Priority and queue-based request handling
  • Transparent request tracking and status updates
  • Secure authentication and controlled access
  • Scalable design for future enhancements

About This System

The Essential Commodity Distribution System is an academic demonstration platform designed to showcase priority-based and queue-driven allocation of critical resources such as LPG, medical oxygen, and fuel during high-demand or emergency situations.

Terms & Privacy

This platform is an academic prototype and does not replace official government portals or authorized supply systems.

User data is utilized solely for request prioritization, queue management, and delivery estimation, and is not shared with third parties.
