Priority Tiers
Allocation is Fair, Not First-Come-First-Served
🔴 Tier 1 — Critical
Hospitals, ICUs, Emergency Services. Guaranteed allocation during crises.
🟡 Tier 2 — Priority
BPL households, elderly (60+), pregnant women, differently-abled individuals.
🟢 Tier 3 — General
Standard general public households. Normal allocation schedule applies.
🔵 Tier 4 — Commercial
Industrial and commercial consumers. Lower priority during scarcity events.
