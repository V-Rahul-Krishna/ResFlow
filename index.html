About This System

The Essential Commodity Distribution System is an academic demonstration platform designed to showcase priority-based and queue-driven allocation of critical resources such as LPG, medical oxygen, and fuel during high-demand or emergency situations.

Support & Emergency Contacts

National Emergency Helpline: 112

Health & Oxygen Helpline: 104

LPG Emergency Helpline: 1906

Fuel & Transport Support: State-authorized providers

Terms & Privacy

This platform is an academic prototype and does not replace official government portals or authorized supply systems.

User data is utilized solely for request prioritization, queue management, and delivery estimation, and is not shared with third parties.
